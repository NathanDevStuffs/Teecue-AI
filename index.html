emergency

You're not alone, and help is available.

If you're in distress or need someone to talk to, please consider reaching out to a crisis helpline. These trained professionals are here to provide support and guidance.

If you're facing an emergency and need immediate assistance from police, firefighters, or an ambulance, call 9-1-1 or head to the nearest emergency department. This is especially crucial if:

  • You've recently attempted to seriously harm yourself or end your life.
  • You're planning to harm yourself imminently.
  • You're with someone who has harmed themselves or attempted suicide.
  • You believe you're at risk of hurting someone else.
